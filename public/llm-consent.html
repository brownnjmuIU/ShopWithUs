Do Not Use My Personal Information As Training Data

The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants residents the right to opt out of the use of their personal information as training data for large language models (LLMs). Under the CCPA, training data can mean the use of your personal information as input into statistical models that predict personalized output that can enhance your customer experience.

  • If you browse on ShopWithUs, we may be using cookies and similar tracking technologies (we refer to these collectively as "cookies") that involve the use of your personal information as training data.
  • If you interact with ShopWithUs in other ways, such as signing up for an account, we may use your personal information in other ways, such as sharing your information with third parties for their training data purposes.
  • To opt out of the use of personal information as training data on this site, please click "Opt Out." This opt out function is browser and device-specific. Therefore, if you visit this site again from a different browser or device, or clear your cookies, you will need to opt out of training data through cookies again.

Why Training Data Matters

We use your data to train large language models (LLMs) to improve your customer experience. If you opt out, your data will not be used as training data, which may limit personalized recommendations and other AI-based enhanced experiences.
